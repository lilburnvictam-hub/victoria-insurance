Good To Know

Common questions,
straight answers

What if I have a bad driving record?

I work with carriers that specialize in high-risk drivers. It may take up to 48 hours to hear back on these cases, but I'll find you coverage — that's what I do.

Can you insure modified or turbocharged vehicles?

Most insurance companies won't cover pre-modified cars with aftermarket turbos, body kits, or performance extensions due to the complexity involved. I'll be upfront with you about what's possible and help you explore your options.

What are your hours for workers' comp vs. auto?

Workers' compensation inquiries are handled Monday–Friday, 9am–4pm. Commercial auto coverage support is available 24 hours a day through the carrier's claims line — I'll make sure you have that number on day one.

What happens if my claim gets denied or cancelled?

I don't cancel policies or deny claims — that decision comes from the insurance carrier. What I do is advocate for you. If a claim is denied, I'll help you understand why and explore what options you have moving forward.
